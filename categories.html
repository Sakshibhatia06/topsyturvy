STAINLESS STEEL PVD

TOPSY TURVY Stainless steel cookware with PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating combines the strength of stainless steel with advanced technology for enhanced performance and aesthetics. PVD coating involves applying a thin, durable layer of metal, such as titanium or ceramic, onto the stainless steel surface through a vacuum process. This coating significantly improves the cookware's resistance to scratches, stains, and tarnishing, ensuring it maintains its sleek, modern appearance over time
