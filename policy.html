Domestic Shipping Policy

We process all orders within 2 to 3 business days. Please note that shipments and deliveries do not occur on weekends or holidays.
During periods of high demand, shipping may be delayed by 7 to 10 business days. We kindly ask for your patience as additional transit time may also be required. If there are any significant delays with your order, we will reach out to you via email or phone.
Typically, domestic shipments are in transit for 2 to 7 business days.
For festive seasons, special occasions, or bulk orders, we recommend placing your orders 10 to 12 days in advance of your desired delivery date to ensure timely arrival.

Additional Points:

  • Shipping Carriers: We partner with reliable carriers to ensure your order is delivered safely and efficiently. You may receive tracking information via email once your order has shipped.
  • Delivery Locations: We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. For remote areas, delivery times may vary, and additional charges may apply.
  • Shipping Costs: Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the weight and dimensions of your order, as well as your delivery location.
  • Order Confirmation: Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email with details about your purchase and estimated delivery time.
  • Package Inspection: Please inspect your order upon arrival. If you notice any damage or discrepancies, contact our customer service team within 48 hours so we can assist you promptly.
  • Returns and Exchanges: Please refer our Returns and exchange policy section for details.

If you have any questions about your order or our shipping policy, feel free to reach out to our customer service team. We’re here to help!

Returns and Exchange Policy

We accept returns exclusively for the following reasons: manufacturing defects, shipping damages, or if the wrong product was received.
If you wish to initiate a return or exchange, please contact us at customer care number or send us a mail at topsyturvyallure@gmail.com with your request. To process your return, you will need to provide the original purchase invoice and include at least two photographs of the product.
For wrong items received, an unboxing video is required to complete the return/exchange request.
Please note that we only accept returns or exchanges for items purchased directly from our website, www.topsyturvy.com, or from our showroom. Products bought from other websites or dealers will be subject to their respective return policies.
Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery. Once we receive the returned product in its original packaging, along with all necessary documentation, we will promptly send you a replacement.

Additional Points:

  • Condition of Products: To qualify for a return, products must be unused and in their original packaging with all tags and accessories included.
  • Return Shipping Costs: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong item sent). In such cases, we will cover the return shipping fees.
  • Refunds: If a refund is approved, it will be processed to the original payment method within a specified timeframe. Please note that the duration may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Certain items, such as personalized or custom products, may not be eligible for return. Please check the product description for details.
  • Tracking Returns: We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance for returns, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
  • Customer Support: Our customer support team is available to assist you throughout the return process. Don’t hesitate to reach out with any questions or concerns.
  • Exchange Process: If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, please indicate this in your return request, and we will facilitate the process once we receive the returned item.

Thank you for your understanding, and please reach out if you have any questions!
